Three-way comparison. Left = currently shipped Tailwind 600. Center = Observable Plot palette (designed by Mike Bostock for data viz). Right = Vega category10 (Tableau-style, used widely in scientific dashboards). Each column applies the palette to a stacked bar, donut, and 4-series line chart.

Chart palette: three options

Same sample data across all three. Pick the one that's easiest on the eyes after 30 seconds of staring.
What to look for
  • Bar stacks: do the 3 account colors separate cleanly?
  • Donut wedges: are smaller slices (right side of donut) still distinguishable?
  • Line chart: can you follow each of 4 lines individually?
  • Aesthetic: which palette would you want to look at every day in a finance dashboard?
  • Approachability: Tailwind 600 feels "tech UI"; Observable feels "premium dataviz"; Vega feels "scientific/Tableau".
