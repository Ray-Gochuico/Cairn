Investments over time
Monthly snapshots · all 3 accounts
Sample data: 24 months of monthly snapshots for 3 accounts. Daily/weekly views synthesize finer-grained data from the same series for visualization purposes.
What the chart shows
  • Stacked bars: each account's value at that bucket end-date, stacked. Bar height = total portfolio value at that point.
  • Line overlay: traces the top of the stacked bars (total over time). Same data as bar-tops; emphasizes trajectory.
  • Granularity buttons: re-bucket the same underlying snapshots. Each bucket takes the LATEST snapshot per account in that bucket.
  • Account multi-select: defaults to all selected. Unchecking drops that account's bar + reduces the total line accordingly.
  • Tooltip on hover: shows per-account values + total for that bucket.
  • Hard cap: 90 bars shown at once. If "Days" exceeds 90 days of data, shows the most recent 90.
